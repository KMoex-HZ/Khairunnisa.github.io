About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate and driven individual with a strong background in data science, multimedia, and education. My journey has been marked by excellence in both academics and extracurricular activities, reflecting my commitment to continuous learning and impactful contributions.

I began my academic journey in multimedia studies at SMKN 1 Bandar Lampung, where I developed a deep passion for creativity and technology. My time there was marked by achievements such as being awarded the Top 1 Outstanding Student Awardee and gaining hands-on experience through industry work in graphic design. As a Teaching Assistant in multimedia, I had the opportunity to guide peers in mastering technical skills, which honed my leadership and mentoring abilities. This foundation set the stage for my transition into a broader field where I could merge creativity with analytical problem-solving.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Data Science at Institut Teknologi Sumatera. Here, I’ve excelled academically, winning a Gold Medal in the English National Competition and a Silver Medal in Mathematics. I’ve also embraced leadership and academic roles, such as being a Teaching Assistant for courses like Pervasive Computing, Multimedia Technology, and Digital Signal Processing, as well as serving as a Computer Lab Assistant. Beyond academics, my involvement in events like Inspiraxpo and Refreshing with HMSD Adyatama as a Secretary has allowed me to sharpen my organizational and teamwork skills. These experiences have equipped me with a unique blend of technical expertise and practical leadership, preparing me for future challenges in data science and beyond.
